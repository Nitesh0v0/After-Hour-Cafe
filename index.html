Our Story

Born in the lanes
of Bhairahawa.

After Hours Cafe started as a four-table corner shop on Siddartha Highway — a place for late-night conversations over strong coffee. Today, it's a beloved community landmark where locals, students, and travelers find warmth, great food, and even better company.

Every bean is sourced from Nepali hillside farms. Every pastry baked fresh each morning. Every cup pulled with obsessive precision.
