Our Story

Born in a corner, brewed with purpose.

How It Started

A corner shop that became a community.

After Hours Cafe was founded in 2019 by a group of coffee enthusiasts in Bhairahawa who believed that great coffee shouldn't stop when the sun goes down. What started as a tiny, dimly-lit 4-table café on Siddartha Highway has grown into a beloved institution in Rupandehi.

We source our beans from hill-grown farms in Nepal and partner with roasters who care as much about the craft as we do. Every cup is pulled with precision and served with warmth.
